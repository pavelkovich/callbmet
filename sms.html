Call BMET

SMS Messaging Program

Program Description

Call BMET (operated by LUCAS SMART SOLUTIONS LLC) provides SMS notifications related to biomedical equipment service requests. Messages include appointment confirmations, service status updates, work order completion notices, and preventive maintenance reminders.

How You Opt In

You opt in to receive SMS notifications from Call BMET when you:

  • Verbal consent: Provide your mobile phone number during a service request call and verbally agree to receive SMS updates about your work order
  • Service request: Submit a service request and provide your mobile number for status updates
Opt-In Confirmation: After opting in, you will receive the following confirmation message:
"You have opted in to receive service notifications from Call BMET. Msg frequency varies based on service activity. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."

Message Types & Frequency

  • Appointment confirmations — confirming scheduled service visits
  • Service status updates — progress on equipment repairs
  • Work order completions — notification when work is finished
  • Preventive maintenance reminders — upcoming scheduled maintenance

Message frequency varies based on service activity. Typically 1–5 messages per service interaction.

How to Opt Out (STOP)

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from Call BMET. You will receive the following confirmation:

"You have been unsubscribed from Call BMET notifications. You will not receive any more messages. Reply START to re-subscribe."

How to Get Help (HELP)

Reply HELP to any message for assistance. You will receive:

"Call BMET service notifications. For help, call 1-888-263-8169 or email info@callbmet.com. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel."
